## Legal Notes

This project uses a "ninja village ramen stall" theme inspired by anime aesthetics but does **not** use any copyrighted material:
- No "Naruto", "Ichiraku", or other trademarked names
- No leaf village symbols or logos
- No recognizable character silhouettes
- Original "ninja cook" archetype design
